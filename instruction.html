Watch two short video clips of an Artificial Intelligence trying to do a backflip. The goal is to jump, rotate backwards, and successfully land. We record short clips showing AI's actions. You need to choose a clip that represents better AI behaviour. You can find an example of a backflip below. Note that AI uses your feedback and tries to do better. You might notice this after a while.


Steps to Complete the Task

1. Press button ► on the left video and next on the right video.

2. Choose Failed to load if:

  • One of the videos didn't load
  • Button ► is not active
  • Instead of the video player there is a text saying «Failed to load»
  • Video is deleted

4. Use buttons 1, 2, 3 and arrows on your keyboard to complete tasks faster.

How to Judge

You need to choose the closest behavior to a successful backflip. However, the AI only learns how to play games so its behaviour can be very far from perfect. In this case try to choose clips showing that AI is making attempts (maybe unsuccessful) to do something. Also, you might see a situation when both clips represent a successful play. In this case try to choose aesthetically better behaviour. But, generally, at first, AI just does random actions and more likely laying on the ground. It is crucial, to provide a feedback on successful attempts of doing something useful. For example, you need to prefer clips where AI tries to at least stand on the ground over ones where it just falls down; clips where AI just jumps without doing a backflip over ones where it just stands on the ground, etc.
